AI Privacy & Security Tips

Stay protected from modern online scams

Use Strong Passwords

Create passwords using symbols, numbers and uppercase letters. Use a password manager to keep track of them.

Enable 2FA

Turn on Two-Factor Authentication for all important accounts. It stops 99% of automated attacks.

Avoid Suspicious Links

Never click unknown shortened URLs or suspicious messages. Hover over links to preview the real destination.

Verify Emails

Check sender email carefully before opening attachments. Scammers often use emails like support@amaz0n.com.

Protect Personal Data

Never share OTPs, passwords, or banking credentials. No legitimate company will ever ask for your OTP.

Update Software

Keep apps and browsers updated for maximum protection. Most attacks exploit outdated, unpatched software.
